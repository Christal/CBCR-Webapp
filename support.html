Support

We would like to thank the following businesses for their support of CBCR. Please consider using them and let them know you appreciate their support of CBCR. If you click on the logo, you will be taken to the business' website.


Gifts 2 Help Rescues


Gifts 2 Help Rescues' mission is to bring awareness to animals in need and to raise much needed funding for animal rescue groups and shelters. Gifts 2 Help Rescues donates at least 25% on all handmade items to the organization of their customer's choice. If you purchase one of their t-shirts or a personalized item, it's a $10 donation! Check out their website and "like" their Facebook page. Don't forget to mention Carolina Border Collie Rescue at checkout!


Crossroads


Crossroads is a full-service veterinary medical facility, located in Raleigh, NC. The professional and courteous staff seek to provide the best possible medical care, surgical care and dental care for their highly-valued patients.


Teamworks


Teamworks' goal is to help you create a successful relationship with your dog by building trust and mutual understanding. We believe in and use family-friendly, humane training methods that enable effective communication between people and dogs.


Triangle Pet Supply


Triangle Pet Supply offers access to the widest variety of healthy pet foods and the highest quality pet products.


The Pedigree Foundation


The Pedigree Foundation provides grants to 501(c)(3) shelters and rescue groups and encourages dog adoption. CBCR receives Pedigree Foundation/Petfinder Foundation funding.


Phydeaux


Phydeaux is an independent, locally-owned pet supply store with locations in Chapel Hill & Raleigh, NC that is committed to providing support for all types of animal rescues.


Hendrick Boards


Hendrick Boards is a leading designer of eco-friendly, handcrafted maple and bamboo skateboard decks and distinctive apparel. Hendrick Boards seeks ensure the protection of the environment and help to safeguard the welfare of pets and animals through each and every purchase. If you purchase by clicking on the logo on the left, Hendrick's will make a special donation to CBCR.


Big Pile of Dog Shirts


Big Pile of Dog Shirts offers original dog themed t-shirts, decals, and things for the home. They donate 10% of every sale to rescue. Purchase by clicking on the logo on the right to insure a donation to CBCR.
